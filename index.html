Welcome to Singh Engineering

SINGH ENGINEERING PRIVATE LIMITED is a premier manufacturer and supplier of high-performance hardware systems, including High-Pressure Water Jetting Machines, Hydraulic Pumps, and Industrial Cleaning Solutions. Our innovation-driven approach and decades of expertise ensure superior product quality and customer satisfaction.

Headquartered in Jamshedpur, Jharkhand and founded in 2025, we are proud to serve clients across India. Led by Mr. Suraj Singh, our journey reflects excellence, innovation, and trust.

Our engineering team is a blend of passion and precision, continuously pushing boundaries to deliver industry-leading solutions.
